The survey is to identify the travel patterns within the campus for developing necessary infrastructure for campus transportation. This is a pilot survey published and managed by the Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering, IIT Jodhpur. All of the information that you provide will be treated as confidential and will only be used for research purposes. Your comments will not be identified as belonging to you, instead they will be combined with those gathered from other survey participants, and will be analysed as part of a group.We do not sell, rent or exchange any personal information supplied by you to any third party. Nor do we use any of the information you provide for direct marketing or other non-research activities.

About Us


Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur (IIT Jodhpur or IITJ), is a public university located in Jodhpur in the state of Rajasthan in India.

IITJ is located about 24 km from Jodhpur city on National Highway 62 which connects Jodhpur to Nagaur.The site spans over 852 acres (3.45 km2) of land around Jhipasni and Gharao villages.

IIT Jodhpur had set up its transit residential campus at Kendranchal colony on New Pali Road, Jodhpur. Faculty and students used to reside in the transit residential campus, which had a dining facility and a health center.
